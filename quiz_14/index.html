What This Means

About the characteristics:

  • Communal characteristics (like caring, supportive, understanding) are traditionally associated with women
  • Agentic characteristics (like dominant, assertive, controlling) are traditionally associated with men

Many people are surprised to find they unconsciously favor traditional "male" leadership traits over "female" ones, even when this conflicts with their stated values. This test reveals unconscious biases that can disadvantage women in leadership roles.

Understanding these biases is the first step toward creating more inclusive leadership environments that value diverse leadership styles.
