Your Leadership Dimension Scores

What Your Scores Mean

Your scores show how you view and define leadership. The way you think about leadership affects how you practice it.

Quick Guide to Each Leadership Dimension:

  • Trait: Leadership comes from special personality traits and natural gifts
  • Ability: Leadership is a learned skill that can be developed over time
  • Skill: Leadership requires specific knowledge, competence, and know-how
  • Behavior: Leadership is about what people do and how they act
  • Relationship: Leadership focuses on communication between leaders and followers
  • Process: Leadership involves leaders, followers, and situations working together

Compare your scores to see which aspects of leadership matter most to you. Your highest and lowest scores reveal your leadership philosophy!
